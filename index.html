How to Play

Your goal is to find all the mines!

Controls

Left Click:

will reveal the tile.

Right Click:

will place or remove a flag.

Middle Click:

will reveal the surrounding tiles.

Icons

Numbered tiles tell you how many mines surrounding that tile.

Flags can be placed on tiles that you think is a mine, but not all mines need to be flagged for you to win

When you incorrectly select a tile that is a mine you automatically lose, revealing all other flags.

When you place a flag on a tile that did not contain a mine. Flags that are correctly marked will stay the same icon.

You're ready to play! Now get out there are start sweeping!
